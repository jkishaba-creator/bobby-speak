# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be decent to each other. This is a small project people work on in their free
time.

## Expected behavior

- Assume good faith. Most disagreements are misunderstandings.
- Critique code, not people. "This will break on Wayland" is useful; "this is
  amateur work" is not.
- Accept that maintainers say no. A closed PR is not an insult — scope is how
  a small project stays maintainable.
- Remember that contributors are volunteers, including when a response takes
  a while.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination or demeaning comments based on who someone is.
- Publishing others' private information.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or deliberately derailing threads.

## Scope

This applies in issues, pull requests, discussions, commit messages, and
anywhere else the project is being represented.

## Enforcement

Report problems to [@jkishaba-creator](https://github.com/jkishaba-creator) by
opening a private security advisory or contacting the maintainer directly.
Reports are handled confidentially.

The maintainer may edit, hide, or delete comments, close PRs and issues, and
block accounts, at their discretion, for behavior that violates this document.
